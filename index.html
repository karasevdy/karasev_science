Biography

I am PhD in sociology since the 2016 (Moscow State University) and practised Data Scientist since the 2023 (Yandex Practicum, Otus). Till the 2024, I was a Research Fellow at the MGIMO University, now I am Senior Research Fellow at The Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy and Public Administration (RANEPA).

Currently, I am working in the field of Social and Humanities Computing: multi-agent modeling and computing in legislative studies; books summarization via LLM + LangChain; vector-based search and modeling using embeddings.

As part of my recent project, it was developed the state-of-the-art multi-agent model of legislative behavior in the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine of the 8th convocation. The general principles of the elaborated and finetuned model being developed the approaches of D. MacRae and K. Poole, H. Rosenthal and can be used to predict the behavior of members of any parliament (for which roll calls are publicly available).
